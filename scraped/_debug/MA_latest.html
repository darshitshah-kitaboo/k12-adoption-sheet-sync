Instructional Support

The Center for Instructional Support works to give every student access to a safe and supportive school environment that cultivates academic curiosity and confidence. The Center's vision for Massachusetts schools is one in which students have equitable access to an excellent education; read meaningful texts across content areas; work on complex, real-world problems; participate in the arts; and share their ideas through speaking and writing using evidence, all in an effort to understand the world and nurture their own identities.
