The curriculum services section provides professional services related to the development and implementation of improved curricula in the schools of the state. The unit engages in a broad range of activities including: 

  • the preparation and dissemination of curricular materials;

  • in-service training for professional staff members in curriculum development and in specific subject areas; and

  • consultative assistance in solving instructional problems. 

Directors provide services upon request in the areas of citizenship education, fine arts, reading, writing, mathematics, social studies, and science. Particular emphasis is placed on assisting schools in aligning curriculum with the Show-Me Standards and the development of written curriculum guides required by the Missouri School Improvement Program and the Outstanding Schools Act.

DESE Summer Learning: Standards, Assessment, and Artificial Intelligence Training

K-12 educators and Administrators are welcome to join the DESE’s Standards, Assessment, and Artificial Intelligence training on July 31 through August 1! This free training is designed to assist educators of all content areas and levels in developing a deeper understanding of the Missouri Learning Standards. Topics will include:

  • Standards Training:
    • Bundling standards in lesson development.
    • Supporting standards in relation to priority standards.
    • Utilizing disciplinary practices to foster deeper student understanding.
  • Assessment Development Training:
    • Understanding and using assessment documents: Item Specifications and Performance Level Descriptors.
    • Evaluating current assessment questions for rigor and informational value.
  • Artificial Intelligence Integration Training:
    • Basics of artificial intelligence and practical uses of ChatGPT.
    • Evaluating ChatGPT outputs against desired standards and rigor.

Training Dates:

  • July 31, 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. (lunch not provided)
  • August 1, 9:00 a.m. to noon

Location:

  • Truman Building 301, W. High Street, Jefferson City, MO, 65101
